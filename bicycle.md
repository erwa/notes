### Tire Numbers

Example: 40-622 (700 x 38C - 28 x 1 5/8 x 1 1/2)

ISO-5775
* 40 is inflated width in mm
* 622 is internal diameter in mm

French size
* 700 mm is outer diamter
* 38 mm is width

Inch size
* 28" outer diameter
* 1 5/8" is height
* 1 1/2" is width
