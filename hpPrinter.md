### Scan to Email

In Gmail security settings, ensure 2FA disabled. Enabled connections from less secure devices.

Then go to 192.168.1.65:
* SMTP: smtp.gmail.com
* Port: 465
* SSL: Checked
* Enter full Gmail address + password
* Send test email.
